# Horoscope Page

## 🧠 The "Ego" Suite (IQ & Logic)

-   **The AI Baseline IQ Test:** A high-tech, 10-minute calibration using AI-generated logic puzzles that change every time (prevents cheating).
    
-   **Cognitive Aura Profile:** Instead of just a number, users get a 13-trait "Brain Map" (e.g., _Spatial Wizard, Verbal Fluidity, Memory Fortress_).
    
-   **Professional Certification:** A high-end, downloadable PDF "Cognitive Identity" certificate designed for LinkedIn or Instagram bragging.
    
-   **The "Brain Age" Scan:** A reaction-time and memory test that tells you if your brain is "younger" or "older" than your physical age.
    


## ✨ The "Oracle" Suite (Identity & Future)

-   **AI Astrology (Co-Star Style):** Hyper-personalized daily "Prophecies" based on real-time planetary movements and the user's natal chart.
    
-   **The Vision Palm Reader:** Users take a photo of their hand; the AI "maps" their palm lines to their IQ and destiny.
    
-   **Love & IQ Compatibility:** A tool that analyzes two people's birth charts and cognitive scores to see if they are a "Power Duo" or "Chaotic Match."
    
-   **Wealth Windows:** A money-focused forecast that tells the user which days of the month their "logic" and "luck" align for financial decisions.
    
-   **The Perfect Partner Blueprint:** A deep-dive questionnaire where users answer 40+ psychological questions about their values, finances, and life goals. The AI then generates a detailed "Soulmate Persona"—a complete profile of who their ideal husband or wife actually is.


## 🚀 The "Viral" Suite (Social Media bait)

-   **The Past Life Reveal:** The AI analyzes the user's face (selfie) and IQ score to generate a historical portrait of who they were (e.g., _15th Century Alchemist_).
    
-   **The Brutal Brain Roast:** A "Mean Mode" where the AI mocks the user's results. _"You have the logic of a god but the attention span of a goldfish."_
    
-   **Manifestation Wallpapers:** The AI generates a custom, minimalist phone wallpaper with "hidden" sigils based on the user's specific IQ and Star Sign.
    
-   **The Dream Decoder:** Users record a voice note of a dream; the AI turns the symbolism into a beautiful, shareable "Dream Art" image.


## 🧠 The "Shadow & Self" Suite (Psychological Deep-Dives)

-   **The "Shadow Self" Audit:** Based on a series of specialized prompts, the AI identifies the user's "Shadow" (repressed traits or hidden motivations). It gives a detailed report on how these traits subconsciously affect their career and love life.
    
-   **Core Value Compass:** A decision-making tool where users rank life scenarios. The AI distills these into their **Top 5 Non-Negotiable Values** (e.g., _Freedom, Security, Impact_), helping them understand why certain jobs or people make them feel drained.
    
-   **The "Attachment Style" Simulator:** A situational roleplay with an AI character. Based on the user's reactions, the AI identifies if they are _Secure, Anxious, or Avoidant_ in relationships and gives a "Healing Roadmap."
    
-   **Cognitive Distortion Detector:** Users can vent about a bad day via voice or text. The AI scans for "Thinking Traps" (e.g., _Catastrophizing_ or _All-or-Nothing thinking_) and instantly provides a "Rational Reframe" to lower stress.
    
-   **The Archetype Portrait:** Using the "Big Five" personality model, the AI assigns the user a futuristic archetype (e.g., _The Architect, The Empath, The Disruptor_). It generates a high-fidelity 2026-style avatar that represents their psychological "essence."
    
-   **Burnout Risk Sensor:** A 60-second check-in that analyzes the user's typing speed, tone of voice, and recent "Cognitive Load" to give a "Burnout %" score and a specific "Recovery Protocol."
